Guidelines to authors for Paper/Extended abstract:

  • Extended Abstracts should not exceed 500 words.
  • Full papers must adhere to a 4000-word limit including an abstract between 200 and 250 words, along with 5-6 keywords.
  • The title page should include the paper title, author(s) name(s), affiliation(s), and the corresponding author's email address.
  • Main documents should be double-spaced, with one-inch margins and consecutively numbered pages.
  • Use 12-point Times New Roman font.
  • Submit papers in both Word document and PDF formats.
  • Failure to comply with formatting guidelines may lead to rejection.
  • Submit plagiarism report along with abstract/full paper/articles
  • Oral and poster presentation also permitted.
